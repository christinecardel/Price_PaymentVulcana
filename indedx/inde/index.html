The 2-person Baia DIY Modular Sauna Kit from Auroom is easy to assemble and exceptionally high quality. Featuring comfortable and attractive Finnish contoured bench seating, the Baia sauna cabin is space and energy efficient, and provides a superior sauna experience.

The Baia is made of long-lasting, sustainably-sourced thermally modified Aspen from northern Europe. This chemical-free thermal modification process – done with only heat and steam – produces a product that is longer lasting than other woods and stands up better to the extreme conditions of a sauna. With its comfortable, ergonomic design and special features, the Baia DIY indoor sauna kit really stands out, thanks to its contoured seating. The Baia sauna cabin is a truly premium-quality small home sauna that offers unbeatable value.

Contoured benches are usually only found in larger saunas, but Auroom offers this added luxury and comfort in a sauna cabin that can fit into smaller spaces in your home.
